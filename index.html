What this prototype is
1
Onboarding IA. The "Try the basics" checklist at the bottom of the Chat sidebar walks a new user through importing memory, connecting tools, and getting the desktop app.
2
Usage limits. A usage ring next to the chat input shows plan limits at a glance, and displays best practice nudges before a user hits a wall.
3
Project adoption. Once a few chats look related, Claude surfaces a project suggestion in the sidebar to nudge users into Projects.
Things to try
  • 1 Click an item in the Try the basics checklist, bottom of the Chat sidebar
  • 2 Open a chat and look at the usage ring next to the input and right click to see the nudge
  • 3 Watch the sidebar - a project suggestion appears after a moment
Toggle the violet ① button (top right) to pin labels directly on these three bets while you explore.
